Personal Life

Jolie’s personal life often attracted at least as much attention as her acting. Her relationship with Pitt became fodder for tabloids, and the birth of the couple’s biological children, Shiloh (2006) and twins Knox and Vivienne (2008), caused a media frenzy. Jolie and Pitt married in 2014, but two years later Jolie filed for divorce. Her humanitarian work also drew interest. In 2001 she was named a Goodwill Ambassador for the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR). After that appointment she traveled to numerous poverty-stricken countries and adopted children from Cambodia and Ethiopia—Maddox and Zahara, respectively. Pitt later adopted the children, and in 2007 the couple adopted a boy, Pax, from Vietnam. In 2013 Jolie made news for having a preventive double mastectomy after discovering mutations in her BRCA1 gene, which increase the odds of developing breast or ovarian cancer.

Angelina Jolie Career Timeline

  • 1991 - (Beginning of career) Interview with Angelina Jolie (Defan) marked the beginning of her career. At the time of the interview, she was starring in many music videos and working as a model. She had played in a few stage plays, but had not yet been in a movie.
  • 1995 - (Angelina's First Big Role) Angelina's first leading role was in the movie "Hackers" This was her first big break as an actress where she began to be noticed.
  • 2000 - (Her Big Academy Award) Award for Best Supporting Actress (Oscars) was Jolie's first and only Academy Award. She had been nominated for many awards before, but this was her first win.
  • 2002 - (First Adoption (Maddox)) Jolie adopted her first child, seven-month-old Maddox Chivan from Cambodia. Jolie decided to apply for adoption after she had visited Cambodia twice, while filming.
  • 2003 - (Angelina Jolie's Sri Lanka Journal) Angelina Jolie went on a week-long mission to Sri Lanka. Jolie visited recent returnees, internally displaced persons and victims of the 20-year civil war. She called on the international community to contribute to the country's recovery.
  • 2004 - (Meeting the love of her life) Angelina Jolie met her current fiance, Brad Pitt, while filming "Mr. and Mrs. Smith." Although Brad was married to actress Jennifer Aniston at the time, he could not help but to fall in love with Angelina (Powell).
  • 2005 - (Adoption of Zahara from Ethiopia Jolie adopted her from Wide Horizons For Children orphanage in Addis Ababa. Shortly after they returned to the United States, Zahara was hospitalized for dehydration and malnutrition. In 2007, media outlets reported Zahara's biological mother was still alive and wanted her daughter back.)
  • 2006 - (Jolie gave birth to a daughter, Shiloh Nouvel) Shiloh was born in Namibia. Jolie sold the first pictures of Shiloh through the distributor Getty Images herself. $4.1 million was made and put toward they Jolie-Pitt charity.
  • 2007 - (Adoption of Pax from Vietnam) Jolie adopted a three-year-old boy from Vietnam, Pax Thien, who was born on November 29, 2003. Pax was abandoned at birth at a local hospital, where he was initially named Pham Quang Sang.
  • 2008 - (Birth of Twins, Knox and Vivienne) Jolie gave birth to a boy, Knox Léon, and a girl, Vivienne Marcheline, by c-section. The rights for the first images of Knox and Vivienne were jointly sold to People and Hello! for $14 million.

Popular Movies

Tomb Raider

Tomb Raider is a really silly film, and that’s just fine. It’s probably not saying much, but it’s one of the greatest video game movies of all time. What makes it so enjoyable though is Angelina Jolie, who plays the titular Lara Croft. The plot concerns the Illuminati and planetary alignment, and it really does feel like a story line that belongs in a video game.

Maleficent

Disney has been doing a lot of live-action retellings of their classic cartoons lately, and many of them simply keep the same exact story line as the original movie with a few changes here or there. But Maleficent is different. Taking the Wicked approach, it tells the story from the “villain’s” perspective, and Angelina Jolie plays said “villain”.
